The Story Behind The Name

M87 is more than a name it's a statement of scale, gravity, and ambition.

Inspired by the Messier 87 galaxy, one of the most powerful and luminous galaxies known, M87 represents a system where everything is drawn toward excellence. At its center lies a force so strong it shapes everything around it just as true talent has the power to influence, inspire, and redefine its space.

Like the stars within M87, every individual we work with carries their own energy, identity, and potential. Alone, they shine. Together, they form something far greater a constellation of talent that moves with purpose and impact.

M87 is built on that idea: bringing exceptional people into one orbit, amplifying their presence, and positioning them where they can be seen, felt, and remembered.

This isn't just a name. It's a universe of talent.
